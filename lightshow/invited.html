It Glows, It Searches—
May 31st, 2017 at midnight

It Glows, It Searches is
a one time night lighting performance in New York City, by the people of New York City.

Like a firefly, a window flashes in patterns that are unique
to itself—it exists as its own optical signal, as well as part of the overall flashing ritual
of the Manhattan night skyline.

During It Glows, It Searches—which is best experienced from Brooklyn Heights Promenade—Manhattan's windows will be choreographed in a skyline—
or environment—of blinking patterns that explore and reflect the human experience amidst the urban landscape.

All participating buildings
will receive guide sheets
with instructions to each resident on when to turn
their lights on—participants
will be given a light switch plate, designed for the event.

We want to thank our sponsors in contributing to this
one-of-a-kind New York City commemoration.

The event is public.
